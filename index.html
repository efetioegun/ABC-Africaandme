Places in Africa

Victoria Falls

Victoria Falls presents a spectacular sight of awe-inspiring beauty and grandeur on the Zambezi River, forming the border between Zambia and Zimbabwe.

Mount Kilimanjaro

Located in Tanzania, Mount Kilimanjaro is the African continent's highest peak at 5,895 meters

National Theatre

The National Theatre, Nigeria is the primary centre for the performing arts in Nigeria.

Pyramids of Egypt

The Egyptian pyramids are ancient masonry structures that were constructed by the ancient Egyptians.
